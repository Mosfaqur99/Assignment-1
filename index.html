Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh's innovation pillar focuses on technology, entrepreneurship, and sustainable development.

Sustainability

Bangladesh's sustainability pillar focuses on resilience, renewable energy, and equity.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh's infrastructure is improving, with growing roads, bridges, and energy projects.

Empowerment

Bangladesh excels in women's empowerment, microfinance, and grassroots development.

The Vision of Future

The Vision of Future emphasizes a tech-driven, sustainable, and inclusive growth strategy. It envisions leveraging innovation, renewable energy, and digital transformation to enhance quality of life, modernize infrastructure, and build a globally competitive economy while addressing climate resilience and socio-economic disparities.

Digital Transformation

Bangladesh 2.0 Digital Transformation accelerates innovation, connectivity, and smart solutions for progress.

Green Energy

Bangladesh aims to generate 40% of its electricity from clean energy sources by 2041. DHAKA TRIBUNE The government is expediting 12GW green power projects.

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 emphasizes smart cities, digital transformation, green energy, and advanced infrastructure development.
